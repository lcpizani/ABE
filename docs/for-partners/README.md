## ABE in one paragraph

ABE is a Telegram-based AI assistant that gives Iowa beginning farmers fast, sourced answers to the questions that shape their operations: Is this rent fair? Will this field pencil out? What programs can I qualify for? Is this disease spreading?

Every answer ABE gives is grounded in a specific source — an ISU Extension publication, a USDA program fact sheet, live USDA market data, or a county-level benchmark from a real dataset. ABE does not estimate or invent numbers. If it does not have a sourced answer, it says so and points the farmer to the right office.

---

## Why this matters for partners

ABE is designed to complement, not replace, what your office does. It handles the questions farmers ask before they call — basic eligibility criteria, rent benchmarks, break-even calculations. By the time a farmer reaches your desk, they have context and can have a more productive conversation.

ABE explicitly tells farmers when a question requires a human: legal questions, formal eligibility determinations, and complex tax matters are referred out, not answered.
